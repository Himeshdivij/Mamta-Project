About Us

With the support of Govt. of Gujarat Health department, a scheme of Mamta card has started into practice since 2005. The main motto of this scheme is to provide good health of pregnant women ,young mothers and her childrens by undergoing regular checkups , providing medicines as required along with suggessions of proper diet.
In the year 2013-14 total more than 15lakh mamta cards have been issued wich helps to the needy people and for the betterment of society.The card also empowers families to make decisions for improved health and nutritional ststus and developing children on continual basis.
Mamta card could be used by the following individual and groups:

  • Family Members

    • For gaining knowledge.
    • For using all available service.
    • For practicing optimal care behaviour.
    • For monitoring and promoting growth and development of children.

  • Village Groups/Women Groups

    • As a discussion tool in the meeting.
    • Monitoring effective service delivery in the area.

  • ANM/ASHA/AWW

    • For edcuating families about optimal health,nutrition and care practices.
    • For regarding information of services.
    • For appropriate referrals.

  • Health and ICDS supervisor for ensuring

    • This card is introduced to the targetted families.
    • Its use is properly explained to the families with support materials.
    • There is effective and effecient delivery of services to the targetted families.
    • To varify ASHA incentive.
